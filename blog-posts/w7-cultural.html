Culture: Values

Sunday, September 21, 2014

    The values I selected for myself are:

    accomplishment, enthusiasm, growth, leadership, meaningful work, reputation., responsibility

    Growth:

    Growth is very important for me to be happy, proud and satisfied. Without growth, you are stagnant and are not gaining anything. I am the type of person who needs to be challenged and to learn to be truly enjoying myself, and with out growth I can get pretty down in the pits.

  • Accomplishment: 5
  • Enthusiasm: 5
  • Growth: 5
  • Leadership: 5
  • Meaningful work: 5
  • Reputation: 5
  • Responsibility: 5


    I always try to live up to these values, because these are what define me and who I am at the core. There are certainly times in which my focus wanes, and those are the times when I get down in life. To get myself out of a bad spot, the best thing for me to do is to revert back to these core values.

    The last time some one asked me for advice was about what type of keyboard they should get. I suppose people don’t ask me for advice very often, or maybe when they do I just don’t recognize it as such.

    These values are what defend me from stereotype threat. It happens, yeah, but because of my focus on these values it is a non-issue for me. If I am feeling down I will have to revert back to these values, just as before.
